Meet the Team

We are currently a small charity, with only 3 members of staff. Find out a little about them below.

Margaret

Margaret comes from an art and textiles background, with a degree in Textile design from Glasgow School of Art where she specialised in weaving.

She has been with the Foundation since September 2018 and has already completed some successful projects with local primary schools. Pictures can be viewed here.

She is also a registered STEM Ambassador meaning that she can be called upon to support Community Groups with Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Maths projects.

Richard

Richard comes to us with a background in teaching. He has a degree in Primary Education and taught for 12 years. He then worked as an electronics engineer repairing all kinds of gadgets from mobile telephones to white goods and everything in between!

He has been with the Foundation since October 2018 and is working on a variety of projects which are being piloted in 5 South Ayrshire schools.

He is currently awaiting confirmation of his Stem Ambassador status, and looks forward to working with schools and groups with their projects.

Fraser

Camera shy Fraser is in charge of admin and finance for the Foundation. He also has the unfortunate job of trying to rein in Margaret and Richard's ideas!
