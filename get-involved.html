Get Involved

You can help us develop the Foundation and Fab Lab by writing a letter of support, making a donation, fundraising or sponsoring the purchase of one of the facilities in our Fab Lab.

100% of all donations and funds raised will go directly to our projects and services as all of our operational and administrative overheads have already been covered by our core supporters.

Any help you feel you can give is hugely appreciated. If you are able to support us in any way or would like more information please get in touch.

MaidSafe Foundation is a registered Scottish charity (SC042030). We welcome any donations which can be made in cash, or by cheque (made out to MaidSafe Foundation) sent to our office in Troon or online, please click this button

Thank you very much!
