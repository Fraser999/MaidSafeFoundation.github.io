Global Projects

Fab Labs around the globe are linked to each other through a digital video network. This allows anyone who uses the Fab Lab the opportunity to work together on ideas and projects with people all around the world, sharing global expertise in the search for local solutions.

At Maidsafe Foundation we passionately believe in helping to make the world more balanced, fair and just. We intend to begin by establishing a Fab Lab and Learning Centre in Scotland.

As we progress, we are keen to support projects further afield, helping alleviate some of the horrendous poverty and injustice around the world, empowering communities to generate opportunities for a better life.

Watch this space.
