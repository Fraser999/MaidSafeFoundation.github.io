Events

From 2nd - 5th May we will be running a series of community consultation events in Scotland using the mobile Fab Lab from the Netherlands.

During this week, there will be a progamme of different events for schools, community and arts groups, businesses and media, allowing everyone to explore how the Fab Lab concept can best serve the Scottish community.

For anyone who is interested in taking a peek at the mobile Fab Lab it will also be on show at the Troon 10k and Children's Fun Run on Wednesday 5th May from 6pm until 8pm.

A few of us will be running for the Foundation at the Troon 10K. If you would like to join us or help fundraise please get in touch! Please find more details about the 10k race here: http://www.scotphotos.co.uk/wordpress/

Come along to the mobile Fab Lab and have a go at printing your very own self designed T-shirt!

If you would like further details about the events please contact us at info@maidsafefoundation.org
